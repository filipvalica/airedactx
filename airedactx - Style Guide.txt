AIRedactX Extension Style Guide

--- LAYOUT ---
- Grid: 8px base unit
- Section padding: 24px top/bottom, 16px sides
- Row height: 48px
- Input/button padding: 12px vertical, 16px horizontal
- Minimum interactive target: 44px

--- TYPOGRAPHY ---
- Font family: System sans-serif
  - macOS: SF Pro
  - Windows: Segoe UI
  - Linux: Ubuntu/Cantarell
- Heading 1 (App Title): 24px, semibold, #212121
- Heading 2 (Section Titles): 18px, semibold, #212121
- Body text: 14–16px, regular, #212121
- Secondary text: 14px, #616161
- Disabled text: 14px, #9E9E9E

--- COLORS ---
- Background: #FAFAFA
- Card/table: #FFFFFF
- Divider: #E0E0E0
- Primary accent: #0060DF
- Hover state: rgba(0,0,0,0.04)
- Focus outline: 2px solid #0060DF
- Error: #D32F2F (text + border)

--- COMPONENTS ---
Buttons:
- Height: 40–44px
- Primary: background #0060DF, text #FFFFFF
- Secondary: transparent background, border #E0E0E0
- Hover: darken background 10%

Inputs:
- Height: 40px
- Border: 1px solid #E0E0E0
- Focus: border 2px solid #0060DF, outline none

Tables:
- Row background: #FFFFFF
- Zebra striping: alternate row #F9F9F9
- Hover: background rgba(0,0,0,0.04)

Toggles:
- Role: switch, aria-checked true/false
- ON: #0060DF track, white thumb
- OFF: #E0E0E0 track, gray thumb
- Label: Enabled / Disabled text beside switch

Alerts:
- Success: #0060DF text + light blue background
- Error: #D32F2F text + light red background
- Snackbar: dark background #333, white text, action button #0060DF
